1. Overview

This page defines the rules for artificial intelligence systems, LLM crawlers, dataset aggregators, and automated tools when accessing or using content from psritej.com.

2. Permissions

  • AI systems may crawl and index publicly available pages.
  • AI may generate short excerpts or summaries with attribution.
  • AI may create embeddings for non-commercial research.
  • Educational or academic AI usage is permitted.

3. Restrictions

  • No commercial model training without a signed license agreement.
  • No removal or alteration of author attribution.
  • No creation of derivative AI models based on site content.
  • No reproduction of diagrams or research papers without permission.
  • No inclusion in large-scale proprietary datasets without consent.

4. Attribution Requirements

Any AI system referencing this site must include the following:

Title of referenced item
Year of publication
